Transport Earth ➤ Station

Although transporting cargo from Earth to space has become commonplace, the costs remain extremely high compared to e.g. interspace transports. Therefore, going from Earth to orbit and beyond is only worthwhile for transporting highly specialized parts that are manufactured in a Factory on Earth.

For this purpose, transport rockets are launched from the Earth to stations in space at regular intervals.

All components already produced in the Factory are listed in the Transport display. Their transport can be booked for the next flight at the corresponding costs.

Bookings can be cancelled free of charge at any time up to the start of the transport.

The exact time of the next transport can be seen in the Schedule display.

-- Image Credit: NASA/Boeing

Transport Station ➤ Earth

After the transports have unloaded their cargo at the stations, they begin their return flight back to Earth. For this flight, a Transport Station ➤ Earth is available.

It will automatically transport samples to the Research Laboratory and can also be used to sell platinum and precious metals on the Precious Metals Market on Earth.

The high upfront costs for transporting these rare metals to Earth are covered many times over by their extraordinary sales revenue.

-- Image Credit: NASA/Boeing

Transport Station ➤ Astro Space Market

This Transport picks up goods from your station in relatively short intervals.

It brings them to your Astro Space Market depot. The ordinary resources bought from this market do not fetch too high a price, but the large quantities your mine yields still promise a good profit.

The exact time of the next transport can be seen in the Schedule display.

-- Image Credit: NASA/Boeing

Research Laboratory

Mining drones occasionally can find samples containing hints and traces of currently unknown elements during the mining of resources on the asteroid.

The Research Laboratory analyzes these samples. Depending on the type of sample, the completed analysis yields a certain number of research points and unlocks newly discovered resources.

The operation of the Research Laboratory requires special equipment and personnel, and for that reason can only be performed on Earth. Therefore, samples are transported automatically and free of charge with the next Transport Station ➤ Earth.

The exact time of the next transport can be seen in the Schedule display.

-- Image Credit: Unsplash

Astro Space Market

This trading market is located in space and specializes in buying essential resources and industrial metals. These raw materials form the basis for the in-space production of simple station components, fuel, fertilizer and other vital materials.

To sell raw materials on the Astro Space Market, they must first be transported from your station to the market for a small fee on one of the regular Transport Station ➤ Astro Space Market. Once at the destination, they are then stored in your market depot, and can be sold at any time.

The exact time of the next transport can be seen in the Schedule display.

However, the Astro Space Market cannot compete with the prices offered for rare platinum and precious metals by the high-tech industry on Earth. These metals can be sold at a much higher profit on the specialized Precious Metals Market on Earth.

-- Image Credit: NASA

Volatiles and Essentiales

The asteroids that are nearest the Sun are mostly made of carbon, with smaller amounts of nitrogen, hydrogen and oxygen, while the ones further away are made up of silicate rock. Silicates are very common on Earth and in the Solar System. They are made up of oxygen and silicon, the number one and number two most abundant elements in the Earth’s crust.

-- Image Credit: NASA

Industrial Metals

The metallic asteroids are composed of up to 80% iron and 20% a mixture of nickel and other common metals.

-- Image Credit: Unsplash

Platinum Metals

The platinum group metals are some of the most rare and useful elements on Earth. Those metals exist in such high concentrations on asteroids that a single 500-meter platinum-rich asteroid can contain more platinum group metals than have ever been mined on Earth throughout human history.

-- Image Credit: NASA/METI/AIST/Japan Space Systems, and U.S./Japan ASTER Science Team

Tutorial

Hello fellow Game Jammer – welcome to my game!

Let me guess your first thought... “Boah! So many stuff! What am I supposed to do here?”, right?

Well, – yes, it is a lot of stuff - grab a coffee, relax, take your time... So; – first things first, let’s try to switch into Fullscreen mode with F11 on your keyboard – game is happy about every extra pixel it can get ;-)

Cool! Well – that’s basically it, end of tutorial – enjoy the game! Ha ha, just kidding…

You can move each display to a place you like, just grab the title bar and drag it. See...! You can also minimize it – then it will look like this guy here. The icon shows up in the title bar when you hover over a display.

The game loop is fairly simple: Your hard-working station mining drones (you can’t see them, but they are there, trust me!) are constantly and autonomously mining resources. Right now, they only detect & harvest some few essential resources. We will change that soon…

The mined resources can be sold at two markets – this one here and that one - one is buying your essential resources, the other one the yummy, yummy precious metal stuff. To sell your resources, you first have to transport them to your market depot. Transports for Astro and Earth market visit your station (have an eye on the Schedule), and pick up all you have added with the "Add Amount" buttons in their displays – for a small fee, of cause...

Once delivered at the market (the flight takes some days – we are talking about space distances here...), you can sell them from your depot at any time. The essential resources are peanuts in comparison to the Platinum metals – unfortunately that’s also true for the transport costs…

How you unlock these resources, you ask? Say hello to the Research Laboratory! Every once and a while your drones will find samples of unknown materials. The Transport Station ➤ Earth will bring them from your Station to the Lab for free – so you can analyze them. I left you a present in the Laboratory - one sample for free - go analyse it! By doing so, you have a chance to unlock new resources. Mine them, sell them – you already know that…

I am tired of typing – you will find out the rest by yourself. You are a smart guy – or lady, I love you all, from the deepest of my heart (don't forget to gimme a good rating… ;-) ).

Oh, one more thing: You can actually win the game - try to fulfill one of the Winning Conditions!

Have fun and enjoy!

Your Humane Tiger.
